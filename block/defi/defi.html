Money

There are many different ways we use our money in the course of our life.

Examples

  • buying goods
  • exchanging our local currency for foreign ones
  • getting a loan
  • investing in a company

All these transactions are facilitated by financial corporations.

Banks

A typical financial corporations is a bank. They are controlled and owned by a small group of shareholders.

How Bitcoin works

Review the lesson

Bitcoin is a collection of concepts and technologies that form the basis of a digital money ecosystem.

Whats does this mean ?

The phrase means that Bitcoin is not just one single thing, but rather a combination of different ideas or concepts

  • decentralization
  • cryptography
  • peer-to-peer exchange
The technologies are
  • blockchain
  • mining
  • digital signatures
But the main idea is that together, these elements create the foundation for an entirely new type of financial system.
In this new financial word money exists in digital form, without needing banks or governments to control it.

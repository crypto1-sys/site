A digital currency called Bitcoin

In the Bitcoin system, the currency units are called bitcoins.

These Bitcoins have a value with two properties

  • Store
  • Transmit

1.Holding value (store)

When someone owns bitcoins, those coins represent a certain amount of economic worth.
It’s similar to having money in your bank account or cash in your wallet.

2.Sending value (transmit)

Value is not just kept — it can also be transferred. If you send bitcoins to another person, you are moving economic worth from yourself to them.

The Bitcoin network records this transfer so everyone agrees on who now owns those bitcoins.

Linear equations

Definition linear equation

First we have n variables \( x_1 , \cdots , x_n \) and b and then we have n coefficients \( a_1,...,a_n \)
The linear equation is : \( a_1 x_1 + a_2 x_2 + \cdots + a_n x_n = b \)

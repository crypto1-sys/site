Anastasy Mate

Definition

Anastasiaâ€™s Mate is a classical checkmating pattern where a knight and a rook (or queen) cooperate to trap the opposing king on the edge of the board.
The knight covers key escape squares, while the rook delivers checkmate, often with the help of a pawn or t he edge of the board restricting the king.

Setup

The enemy king is usually on g8 (or h8). A knight on e7 controls the crucial squares g6 and f5, preventing the king from escaping. A rook (or queen) comes to the h-file (for example, Rh7#) to deliver the final blow. The king is trapped against the edge of the board by its own pawn structure (e.g., pawns on g7 and h7).
